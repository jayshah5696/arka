# Research: Openly Accessible Text-Native PDFs for GTM + AI/ML Sales/Marketing Demo

## Summary

Two PDFs stand out as ideal demo inputs for a PDF-grounded generation demo—both are openly accessible (no login wall), text-native (not scanned images), substantive, and directly at the intersection of go-to-market strategy and AI/ML in sales and marketing. A third is included as a strong alternative.

## Top Recommendations

### 1. **BCG — "The Future of Sales with AI" (Executive Perspectives, Aug 2024)**

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **URL** | `https://media-publications.bcg.com/BCG-Executive-Perspectives-Future-of-Sales-with-AI-EP2-5Aug2024.pdf` |
| **Pages** | 23 |
| **Characters** | ~30,000 |
| **Format** | Text-native PDF (originally a PowerPoint-to-PDF export) |
| **Access** | Fully public, no login/gate. Stable BCG media-publications CDN URL. |

**Why it's a great demo input:**
- **Rich structured content** — Contains an executive summary, six market trends, a sales evolution framework (augmented → assisted → autonomous selling), concrete ROI figures (1.8× margin impact), detailed use-case examples with real metrics (e.g., "54% uplift in sales", "30–40 new cross-sell leads per rep"), team composition diagrams, and a step-by-step implementation guide.
- **Dense with extractable facts** — Numbers, frameworks, role descriptions, and specific AI use cases (Sales Avatar, RFP Responder, Sales Coaching, Autonomous Chatbot) make it ideal for grounded Q&A, summarization, or structured extraction demos.
- **Prestigious source** — BCG brand lends credibility to any demo.
- **Moderate length** — 23 pages is long enough to be non-trivial but short enough to process quickly.

### 2. **McKinsey — "AI-Powered Marketing and Sales Reach New Heights with Generative AI" (May 2023)**

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **URL** | `https://www.mckinsey.com/~/media/mckinsey/business%20functions/marketing%20and%20sales/our%20insights/ai%20powered%20marketing%20and%20sales%20reach%20new%20heights%20with%20generative%20ai/ai-powered-marketing-and-sales-reach-new-heights-with-generative-ai.pdf` |
| **Pages** | 11 |
| **Characters** | ~19,000 |
| **Format** | Text-native PDF |
| **Access** | Fully public, no login/gate. Standard McKinsey media URL. |

**Why it's a great demo input:**
- **Concise and focused** — 11 pages covering gen AI's impact on marketing/sales across the full customer journey (top-of-funnel → sale → retention).
- **Survey data with exhibits** — Includes charts on "estimated impact of use cases" with percentage breakdowns (e.g., 60% for lead identification, 55% for marketing optimization), plus adoption statistics ("90% of commercial leaders expect to use gen AI often").
- **Actionable frameworks** — Contains a "Six No-Regrets AI Strategies" playbook and a clear "gen AI sales use case" walkthrough.
- **Key stat for grounding tests** — "Revenue uplift of 3–15% and sales ROI uplift of 10–20%" — perfect for testing whether a grounded system can accurately cite specific claims.
- **Shorter doc** — Good for quick demos or as a second document in a multi-PDF retrieval test.

## Gaps

- All three recommended PDFs are from major tech/consulting brands. If a more "academic" or "government" source is needed (e.g., for neutrality), the [Springer open-access paper on gen AI in marketing](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11747-024-01064-3) could be fetched as a supplementary PDF, though it's a journal article rather than a strategy report.
- The BCG and McKinsey PDFs are from 2023–2024, not 2025–2026. If recency is critical, the Adobe PDF (appears to be 2025) or searching for 2025 Gartner/Forrester open-access summaries would be the next step.
