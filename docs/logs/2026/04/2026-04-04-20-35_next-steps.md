## What the generator stage needs to do

1. Take seed `ConversationRecord`s as input (instructions from seeds, or just seeds as topic inspiration)
2. For each seed: call the LLM to generate a new `(instruction, response)` pair — or `N` pairs per seed via `generation_multiplier`
3. Output `ConversationRecord`s with `source.type = "generated"` and lineage pointing back to the seed
4. Hit the volume shape correctly:
   - `config.generator.target_count * config.generator.generation_multiplier` going into filters
   - `config.filters.target_count` / final target coming out after filters

## Thin-slice first version

The simplest first version should be:
- take each seed instruction
- ask the LLM to generate a similar but different instruction
- generate a response to it
- one round only
- no personas yet
- no Evol-Instruct operators yet

That is the right thin slice. Evol-Instruct-style operators come after the base generator stage is working.
